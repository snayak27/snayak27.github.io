SAP Joule & Business AI

SAP's answer to the AI era — and its biggest 2026 bet. Joule started as a copilot you talk to; now it's a fleet of autonomous agents that don't just answer questions, they do the work.

🤖 The analogy

If Joule the copilot is a helpful assistant who answers your questions, the new Joule agents are a team of specialists who actually finish the tasks — reconciling accounts, matching invoices, resolving exceptions — while you supervise.

❓ The problem it solves

General AI (like ChatGPT) doesn't know your business — your accounts, your processes, your rules. SAP's advantage is 50 years of business data and process knowledge. Joule grounds AI in that context so it gives accurate, compliant, secure answers — not plausible guesses.

🧩 The layers (2026)

  • Joule — the copilot embedded across every SAP app; ask in plain language
  • Joule Studio — build & govern your own AI agents (free design-time through end of 2026)
  • Joule Work — the new way to work: describe an outcome, agents orchestrate it
  • SAP Autonomous Suite — 200+ agents and 50+ assistantsVerified from SAP Sapphire 2026 keynote: "more than 200 agents and over 50 assistants" across 5 domains. across Finance, Spend, Supply Chain, HCM & CX
  • SAP Knowledge Graph — the context layer that stops AI hallucinating about your data

👤 Who uses it  ·  🔗 How it connects

  • Who: everyone — every role gets domain assistants; developers build custom agents in Joule Studio
  • Connects: runs on the SAP Business AI Platform (BTP + Business Data Cloud + AI Foundation); governed centrally by the SAP AI Agent Hub
  • Open: embeds partners like n8n & Vercel; supports MCP and agent-to-agent (A2A) standards

🌍 Real-world example

Month-end close used to take a finance team two weeks of manual reconciling. The Autonomous Close assistant now does the repetitive journal entries and reconciliations itself, flagging only the exceptions for a human to judge — compressing the close from weeks to days.
