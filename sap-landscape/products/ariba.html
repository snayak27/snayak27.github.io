SAP Ariba

SAP's procurement powerhouse. Ariba runs the entire "source-to-pay" journey and connects buyers to millions of suppliers through the SAP Business Network.

🛒 The analogy

Ariba is the governed marketplace for company spending — like a corporate Amazon where suppliers list, buyers compare and purchase, and every transaction is approved, tracked and auditable.

❓ The problem it solves

Without it, companies suffer "maverick spend" (buying off-contract), invisible supplier risk, and invoices that don't match orders. Ariba puts all spending through one controlled, transparent flow — saving money and reducing risk.

🧩 What's inside

  • Strategic Sourcing — RFQs, auctions, awarding
  • Supplier Lifecycle & Performance — 360° supplier profiles & risk
  • Contract Management — AI clause analysis, renewal alerts
  • Buying & Invoicing — guided buying, 3-way match
  • Agentic Intake Management — AI agents classify & route requests

👤 Who uses it  ·  🔗 How it connects

  • Who: the CPO and procurement teams; sits in the spend family with Fieldglass & Concur
  • Connects: approved invoices post to S/4HANA finance; part of SAP Business Network; bridged via BTP

🌍 Real-world example

A manufacturer needs 10,000 tonnes of steel. In Ariba, procurement runs an online auction among approved suppliers, awards the best bid, signs the contract, and — when the steel arrives — the system matches delivery to order to invoice and triggers payment. All on one auditable trail.
