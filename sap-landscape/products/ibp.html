SAP IBP

Integrated Business Planning — the brain of the supply chain. IBP predicts demand, balances supply, and optimizes inventory so the right products are in the right place before anyone runs out.

🚚 The analogy

IBP is the supply chain war room — it watches demand signals and supply constraints together, runs dozens of "what-if" scenarios, and recommends the best plan in minutes instead of weeks.

❓ The problem it solves

Plan too optimistically and you drown in unsold stock; too cautiously and you lose sales to empty shelves. IBP uses real-time data and AI to strike the balance — and to react fast when a supplier fails or demand spikes.

🧩 What's inside

  • Demand Planning — ML-driven forecasting
  • S&OP — Sales & Operations Planning (align demand & supply)
  • Supply & Response — react to disruptions
  • Inventory Optimization — right safety-stock levels
  • Built on HANA Cloud for real-time simulation

👤 Who uses it  ·  🔗 How it connects

  • Who: the COO, supply chain planners & demand managers
  • Connects: plans feed execution in S/4HANA; works alongside Digital Manufacturing, EWM & TM in the supply chain family

🌍 Real-world example

A consumer-goods company sees a heatwave forecast. In IBP, planners simulate a demand surge for cold drinks, instantly see which plants and warehouses can cover it, and adjust the supply plan — getting extra stock to the right regions before the shelves empty.
