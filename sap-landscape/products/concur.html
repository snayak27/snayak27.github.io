SAP Concur

The one most employees actually touch. Concur handles business travel and expenses — booking trips, snapping receipts, and getting reimbursed without the paperwork pain.

✈️ The analogy

Concur is like a self-filing assistant for work expenses — snap a receipt on your phone, it reads it, checks the policy, routes the approval, and posts it to finance, all on its own.

❓ The problem it solves

Expense reports used to mean stapled receipts, manual spreadsheets, and weeks of waiting for reimbursement. Concur digitizes the whole thing — capture, policy-check, approve, reimburse, and book to the ledger — automatically.

🧩 What it does

  • Travel booking — flights, hotels, cars in policy
  • Expense — OCR receipt capture, real-time policy compliance
  • Reimbursement + corporate card reconciliation
  • Editions: Small Business, Standard, Professional, Premium

👤 Who uses it  ·  🔗 How it connects

  • Who: every traveling employee; managed by finance & the CPO
  • Connects: reimbursements post automatically to S/4HANA finance; part of the spend family with Ariba & Fieldglass

🌍 Real-world example

A consultant flies to a client, photographs the taxi and hotel receipts in the Concur app on the way home. Concur reads each receipt, flags anything over policy, routes it to her manager for one-tap approval, and the reimbursement plus the GL posting happen without anyone touching a spreadsheet.
