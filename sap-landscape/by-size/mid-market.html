SAP for mid-market

Growing companies need real ERP power without a multi-year project. GROW with SAP gives them modern cloud ERP with best-practice processes, live in weeks.

GROW with SAP is the serviced apartment of ERP — fully furnished with SAP's proven processes, move in fast, scale cleanly as you grow.

  • Best-practice processes pre-built for finance, supply chain, HR

  • 20+ AI assistants from day one (2026)

🎬 A company in this tier

A 600-person Bangalore electronics manufacturer outgrows its small accounting tool. With no SAP history, it picks GROW: finance, procurement and inventory go live on standardized S/4HANA Public Cloud within weeks, AI assistants help from day one, and upgrades happen automatically. They trade deep customization for speed and predictable cost.

SAP now positions GROW for midsize AND large companies — not just classic mid-market. Many bigger firms that want speed and standardization (not heavy customization) now choose GROW over RISE.
