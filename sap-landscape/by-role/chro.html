The people leader's view

SAP for a CHRO

If you lead HR, your SAP world has one big name — SuccessFactors — covering the entire life of an employee, from the job advert to the farewell party.

👥 Your relationship with SAP

For a CHRO, SAP is the system of record for every person in the company — one trustworthy place for who works here, what they do, what they're paid, and how they're growing.

😣 Your pain points

  • Employee data scattered across countries, systems and spreadsheets
  • Hiring is slow; the best candidates slip away
  • Hard to see skills gaps, flight risks, or who's ready for promotion
  • Payroll must be perfect and compliant in every country you operate

🧰 The SAP products in your orbit

Almost everything sits inside SAP SuccessFactors, a suite of modules:

  • Employee Central — the system of record (org structure, roles, master data, 100+ countries)
  • Recruiting & Onboarding — sourcing, interviews, digital onboarding
  • Learning — training, skill-gap analysis, compliance courses
  • Performance & Goals + Compensation + Payroll
  • SAP Fieldglass — for the external workforce (contractors, temps)

🤖 What's new: AI in HR

With Joule embedded, you can ask "show me employees at flight risk in my team" in plain language. And the 2026 Autonomous HCM agents handle routine tasks — leave requests, onboarding steps, performance check-ins — automatically.

🔗 How it connects

SuccessFactors doesn't live alone. Headcount and salary data flow into finance (S/4HANA) for cost planning. Contractor data from Fieldglass joins employee data for total workforce planning. The connections run through BTP Integration Suite.
