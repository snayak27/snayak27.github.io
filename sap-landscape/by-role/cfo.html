The finance leader's view

SAP for a CFO

If you run finance, SAP isn't an abstract platform — it's the system that closes your books, reports your numbers, and proves your controls. Here's exactly which parts of SAP live in your world.

💼 Your relationship with SAP

For a CFO, SAP is the single source of financial truth. Every sale, cost, payment and asset flows into one place — so when the board asks "what are the numbers?", there's one answer, not five spreadsheets that disagree.

😣 Your pain points

  • The financial close takes too long — weeks when it should take days
  • Numbers live in disconnected systems and never quite reconcile
  • Auditors want airtight, traceable controls (SOX, IFRS, local GAAP)
  • The board wants real-time insight, not month-old reports

🧰 The SAP products in your orbit

  • S/4HANA Finance (FICO) — the core. The Universal JournalUniversal Journal = one single table in S/4HANA holding all financial postings — finance and controlling unified, no reconciliation needed. unifies financial and management accounting in one place.
  • Group Reporting — consolidate many entities into one set of group financials
  • SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC) — dashboards, planning (FP&A), and forecasting
  • SAP Concur — employee travel & expense, auto-posted to your ledger
  • Specialized finance — Treasury, Revenue Accounting (IFRS 15), Tax Compliance

🖐️ What you actually touch day to day

You won't be configuring the system — that's the consultant's job. As CFO you live in the FioriSAP Fiori = SAP's modern app interface — clean tile-based screens instead of the old grey transaction codes. dashboards: cash position, P&L, close status, overdue receivables. Increasingly you just ask — Joule lets you type "show me margin by region this quarter" in plain language.

🧩 The BTP architect lens

Here's where finance + technology meet — and where the rare, valuable people sit. Every finance pain point is a candidate for a BTP extension:

  • Month-end close → automate repetitive steps with Build Process Automation
  • Dispute/collections → a custom approval app in Build Apps
  • Cash application → an AI/ML matching extension on BTP

Understanding both the finance process and how to extend it on BTP is exactly the techno-functional profile that's scarce in the market.
