Process · Hire to Retire

The HR process

Every employee travels the same arc — hired, onboarded, paid, developed, and one day moved on. SAP SuccessFactors has a module for each step.

👥 What this process is

Hire to Retire = the complete life of an employee inside a company, from the very first job posting to their final day — all on one connected people platform.

The flow, step by step

1
Attract & hire
Post the job, source candidates, run structured interviews, make the offer.
👥 SuccessFactors Recruiting
2
Onboard
Digital onboarding journey — paperwork, equipment, first-week plan, before day one.
👥 SuccessFactors Onboarding
3
Record & manage
The person becomes a record — role, org position, master data — the HR single source of truth.
👥 SuccessFactors Employee Central
4
Pay
Run payroll accurately and compliantly across countries; costs flow to finance.
👥 SuccessFactors Payroll → 📦 S/4HANA
5
Develop & reward
Goals, performance reviews, learning paths, compensation and succession planning.
👥 Performance · Learning · Compensation
6
Plan the workforce
Analyze headcount, attrition risk, skills gaps — including external contractors.
👥 Workforce Analytics + Fieldglass

Before vs after SAP

Without one system

  • Employee data scattered per country
  • Slow, manual hiring and onboarding
  • No visibility on skills or flight risk
  • Payroll errors and compliance gaps

With SuccessFactors

  • One global system of record
  • Digital, fast hire-to-onboard
  • AI surfaces risks and gaps
  • Compliant payroll in 100+ countries
