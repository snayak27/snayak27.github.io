Process · Source to Pay

The procurement process

Every rupee a company spends follows a path — find a supplier, agree a deal, order, receive, pay. SAP Ariba governs the whole journey.

🛒 What this process is

Source to Pay = the full buying journey, from "we need to buy something" all the way to "the supplier has been paid" — with every step controlled, approved and recorded.

The flow, step by step

1
Find & source suppliers
Run RFQs and auctions, compare bids, pick the best supplier for the category.
🛒 Ariba Strategic Sourcing
2
Manage suppliers & risk
360° supplier profiles — performance, risk, compliance, segmented by category.
🛒 Ariba Supplier Lifecycle
3
Contract
Draft, negotiate and store contracts with AI-assisted clause analysis and renewal alerts.
🛒 Ariba Contract Management
4
Buy (guided)
Employees buy from approved catalogues; requests are classified and routed by AI agents.
🛒 Ariba Buying · Agentic Intake
5
Receive & match
Goods arrive; the system 3-way-matches order, delivery and invoice automatically.
📦 S/4HANA + 🛒 Ariba Invoicing
6
Pay
Approved invoices post to finance and the payment run pays the supplier.
📦 S/4HANA Finance (AP)

Before vs after SAP

Without one system

  • Buying outside agreed contracts ("maverick spend")
  • Supplier risk invisible until it's a problem
  • Invoices that don't match orders
  • Slow, email-based approvals

With SAP Ariba

  • Governed marketplace, controlled spend
  • Live supplier risk & performance
  • Automated 3-way invoice matching
  • AI agents route and resolve exceptions
