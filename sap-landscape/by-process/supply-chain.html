Process · Plan to Deliver

The supply chain process

Getting a physical product to a customer is a chain of steps — forecast demand, plan supply, make it, store it, ship it. Each link has its own SAP product.

🚚 What this process is

Plan to Deliver = from predicting what customers will want, to having it made and delivered on time — without drowning in excess stock or running out.

The flow, step by step

1
Forecast demand
Predict what customers will buy, using history and AI/ML signals.
🚚 SAP IBP · Demand Planning
2
Plan supply (S&OP)
Balance demand against capacity and inventory; optimize safety stock.
🚚 SAP IBP · S&OP + Inventory
3
Make it
Control the factory floor — work instructions, quality, machine efficiency (OEE).
🏭 SAP Digital Manufacturing
4
Store it
Warehouse tasks — slotting, picking, packing — optimized inside the ERP.
📦 SAP EWM (in S/4HANA)
5
Ship it
Plan freight, choose carriers, settle costs, track-and-trace to the door.
🚛 SAP Transportation Management
6
Maintain assets
Keep machines running — AI agents pre-diagnose faults and pre-fill work orders.
🤖 Autonomous Asset Management

Before vs after SAP

Without one system

  • Forecasts guessed in spreadsheets
  • Too much or too little stock
  • Factory issues found out too late
  • No end-to-end visibility

With SAP

  • AI-driven demand forecasting
  • Optimized inventory, freed-up cash
  • Real-time shop-floor control
  • Plan-make-move in one connected loop
