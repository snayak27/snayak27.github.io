Process · Record to Report

The finance process

"Record to Report" is the heartbeat of finance — capturing every transaction and turning it into trustworthy financial statements. Here's each step, and the SAP product behind it.

💰 What this process is

Record to Report (R2R) = from the moment money moves, to the moment the company can confidently say "here are our numbers." Recording → adjusting → closing → consolidating → reporting.

The flow, step by step

1
Record the transaction
A sale, a purchase, a payment happens. It posts instantly to the single finance table.
📦 S/4HANA Finance · Universal Journal
2
Capture employee expenses
Travel and expense claims are captured, policy-checked, and posted to the ledger automatically.
✈️ SAP Concur
3
Adjust & accrue
Period-end adjustments — accruals, provisions, allocations — so the period reflects reality.
📦 S/4HANA Finance (FICO)
4
Close the period
Reconcile, validate, lock the books. The famous "month-end close" — increasingly AI-assisted.
🤖 Autonomous Close (Joule)
5
Consolidate the group
Combine many legal entities into one set of group financials, eliminating intercompany noise.
📦 S/4HANA Group Reporting
6
Report & analyze
Dashboards, statutory reports, plans and forecasts for the board and regulators.
📊 SAP Analytics Cloud

Before vs after SAP

Without one system

  • Numbers in many spreadsheets that disagree
  • Close takes weeks of manual reconciling
  • No real-time view; reports are stale
  • Audit trail is fragile

With SAP

  • One single source of financial truth
  • Close compressed to days, AI-assisted
  • Real-time dashboards on demand
  • Fully traceable, audit-ready controls
