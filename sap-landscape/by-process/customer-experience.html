Process · Lead to Cash

The customer process

Winning and keeping customers is its own journey — from a stranger noticing your brand to a paying, loyal customer. SAP's CX suite covers the front; S/4HANA closes the loop.

💬 What this process is

Lead to Cash = from a marketing spark, through the sale, to the money landing in the bank — and the service that keeps the customer coming back.

The flow, step by step

1
Market & attract
Run campaigns, personalize outreach, generate leads across channels.
💬 SAP Emarsys · Customer Data Platform
2
Sell
Manage the pipeline, score opportunities, close deals — with mobile field sales.
💬 SAP Sales Cloud
3
Transact (commerce)
B2B/B2C storefront — catalogue, pricing, cart, checkout, order.
💬 SAP Commerce Cloud
4
Fulfill the order
The sale becomes a sales order in the ERP, triggering delivery and billing.
📦 S/4HANA Order Management
5
Bill & collect cash
Invoice the customer, recognize revenue, collect payment.
📦 S/4HANA Finance (AR)
6
Serve & retain
Omnichannel support; AI agents resolve queries and keep customers loyal.
💬 SAP Service Cloud

Before vs after SAP

Without one system

  • Marketing, sales & service don't share data
  • Customer re-explains themselves each time
  • Orders re-keyed between systems
  • No single customer view

With SAP CX + S/4HANA

  • One connected customer record
  • Sale flows straight into the ERP
  • No re-entry, no reconciliation lag
  • AI-assisted service at every touch
