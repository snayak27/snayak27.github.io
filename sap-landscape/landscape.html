🧩 SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) The foundation — integration, app-building, automation & AI services that hold everything together
🏗️ Think of it like a building

BTP is the foundation and plumbing. The applications are the rooms where work happens. Business Data Cloud is the wiring that connects every room. And Business AI is the smart assistant that walks the building and does jobs for you. Same building, four ways to look at it.

How it all connects

There's no single hub everything orbits. The honest picture is three layers: the applications (S/4HANA plus the line-of-business suites, each its own cloud product), the BTP platform that connects and extends them, and Joule — one AI copilot across all of it.

The suites — SuccessFactors, Ariba, Concur, CX — each integrate with S/4HANA but can also run without it. BTP is the connective layer, not the host.
