The biggest decision in any SAP project

Where does SAP run?

Before "which products," every company answers a more basic question: where does the software physically live? On our own servers? In a private cloud? In a shared cloud? A mix? This one choice shapes cost, speed, control, and how much you can customize.

🏠 The simplest way to think about it

It's like deciding where to live. On-premise = you own the house (full control, you fix the plumbing). Private cloud = you rent a whole house, landlord maintains it. Public cloud = a serviced apartment, fully furnished, move in today. Hybrid = you keep the family house but rent an apartment in the city too.
