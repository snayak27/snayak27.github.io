Public Cloud — GROW with SAP

The fastest, most standardized way to run SAP. A shared, multi-tenant cloud with pre-built best-practice processes — you adapt to SAP's proven way, and go live in weeks instead of years.

☁️ In one sentence

GROW is a fully furnished serviced apartment — move in today, everything works, but you don't get to knock down walls. The standardization is the speed.

📦 What it actually means

GROW with SAPGROW with SAP = a bundle built around S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition — standardized, AI-enabled ERP across finance, supply chain & HR, designed for fast go-live. is built around S/4HANA Cloud, Public Edition:

  • Multi-tenant — you share the underlying cloud with other customers (your data stays isolated)
  • Pre-configured best-practice processes — SAP's proven way of doing finance, supply chain, HR, out of the box
  • BTP credits + an AI-enabled toolchain to go live in weeks
  • 2026 update: 20+ AI assistants from day one

🎯 Who it's for

GROW targets companies new to SAP Cloud ERP. Important 2026 update: SAP now positions GROW for midsize AND large companies — not just mid-market.

  • No legacy SAP baggage to migrate — start clean on the modern platform
  • Want speed and predictable cost over deep customization
  • Happy to adopt standard processes (this is called Fit-to-StandardFit-to-Standard = adapt your business to SAP's best-practice process, instead of customizing SAP to your old way.)

🎬 A day in its life

A fast-growing Bangalore electronics company outgrows its spreadsheets and small accounting tool. It has no SAP history. It picks GROW with SAP: within weeks, finance, procurement and inventory all run on standardized S/4HANA Public Cloud processes, with AI assistants helping from day one. Upgrades happen automatically every quarter. The CFO trades "build exactly what we want" for "live now, scale cleanly."

🚀 How it goes live so fast: SAP Activate

GROW deployments use SAP ActivateSAP Activate = SAP's cloud adoption methodology with ready-made best practices and guided steps., a 6-phase methodology: Discover → Prepare → Explore → Realize → Deploy → Run. The "Explore" phase is the famous Fit-to-Standard workshop — where the business sees SAP's standard process and decides what (little) to adjust.
