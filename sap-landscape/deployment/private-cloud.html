Private Cloud — RISE with SAP

Your own private, dedicated copy of SAP in the cloud — but SAP (and a hyperscaler like AWS or Azure) runs the infrastructure for you. The standard route for established companies moving off on-premise.

🔒 In one sentence

RISE is renting an entire house — it's yours alone, you can renovate a lot, but the landlord handles the roof, the wiring, and the boiler.

📦 What it actually means

RISE with SAPRISE with SAP = a bundled subscription: S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition + cloud infrastructure + tools + SAP-managed operations, in one contract. is a single bundled subscription. You get:

  • S/4HANA Cloud, Private Edition — a dedicated tenant just for your company (not shared with others)
  • Cloud infrastructure on a hyperscaler (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • SAP BTP credits — to build extensions the clean-core way
  • SAP Business Network access + SAP-managed cloud operations
  • 2026 update: contractual commitment to activate 3 Joule AI Assistants in year one

🎯 Who it's for

RISE is aimed squarely at existing SAP customers transitioning from on-premise — typically large enterprises with years of customization they don't want to lose.

  • You keep much of your flexibility and many existing processes
  • You stop owning hardware and stop running upgrades yourself
  • It's a "modern, tailored transformation" — change without starting from scratch
  • For the most complex migrations, SAP brings partners like Palantir and Accenture

🎬 A day in its life

A global pharmaceutical company has run SAP on-premise for 20 years with hundreds of customizations. The 2027 deadline forces a decision. They choose RISE: SAP migrates their system into a dedicated private cloud tenant on Azure. Their finance team barely notices the change in daily work — but now upgrades are SAP's job, the AI features arrive automatically, and new custom apps get built on BTP instead of hacking the core.

⚖️ RISE vs GROW — the key difference

This is the most common confusion for beginners, so here it is plainly:

  • RISE = private cloud, for existing SAP customers migrating. More customization, "your own house."
  • GROW = public cloud, for companies new to SAP Cloud ERP. Standardized, faster, "serviced apartment."
