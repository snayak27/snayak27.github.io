Browser Telepathy

A browser automation and AI web intelligence bridge — gives AI real-time access to your live browser session via CDP and WebSocket, including authenticated pages behind login that no external scraper can reach.

Imagine this…

A story a 10-year-old would understand

"Imagine if your AI assistant could look over your shoulder and see exactly what's on your screen — your open tabs, what you're reading, even pages where you're already logged in."

Normal AI tools can only browse the public internet. They can't see your Naukri job feed, your LinkedIn messages, or anything behind a login screen.

BraveBridge connects Claude directly to your running Brave Browser. The AI can read the current tab, navigate to a URL using your real session, or silently harvest every URL you visit for later indexing.

In technical terms: a Chrome Extension (WebSocket client :9877) + Python server (SSE :9878) + HIVE gateway proxy. Ring 4 in the HIVE architecture.

What Claude can do with your browser

📖
Read any open tab
brave_get_markdown() returns the current page as clean text — even pages behind login like Naukri job listings or LinkedIn profiles.
🧭
Navigate using your session
brave_navigate() opens URLs in your real browser with your existing cookies — no re-login, no CAPTCHA, no bot detection.
🌾
Passive URL harvesting
Every URL you visit gets silently buffered and flushed to the Organic URL Index every 60 seconds. Your browsing builds the search index automatically.
📸
Screenshots on demand
brave_screenshot() captures what's currently visible. Claude can analyze layout or verify that a navigation succeeded.
⌨️
Click and fill forms
brave_click() and brave_fill() let Claude interact with page elements using your real authenticated session.
📚
Backfill 90 days of history
brave_enrich_history(days=90) processes your browser history and seeds the Organic URL Index instantly.
